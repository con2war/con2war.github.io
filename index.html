Keeranglen House...

Welcome to our charming 2-bedroom retreat in the heart of Pomeroy, Co. Tyrone! Nestled in the tranquil countryside, our property offers a serene escape with breathtaking landscapes that paint the perfect backdrop for your getaway. Situated in Pomeroy village, you'll find yourself immersed in the local charm, with an array of inviting bars, restaurants, cafes, and shops just a stone's throw away. If you're in the mood for a bit more hustle and bustle, the vibrant town center of Omagh is a mere 30-minute drive, offering a delightful mix of activities and amenities.

Welcome to Luxury Glamping!

Craig View Luxury Glamping Pods, nestled in the stunning countryside of Co. Tyrone, Northern Ireland. With its prime location overlooking the majestic Craig mountain, our glamping site offers a perfect getaway for nature lovers and those seeking a unique, tranquil escape. At Craig View, we specialize in providing unforgettable overnight staycations in our cozy and well-equipped glamping pods. Immerse yourself in the beauty of the surrounding natural landscape while enjoying the comforts of modern amenities. Each pod is thoughtfully designed to create a comfortable and stylish retreat, ensuring a truly exceptional glamping experience.

Natural Spring Water Hot Tubs!

>

Our self-catering glamping pods are designed with your convenience in mind. Embrace the freedom of creating your own culinary delights in our fully equipped kitchenettes or take advantage of the option to order delivery from local restaurants, allowing you to savor the flavors of the region without leaving the comfort of your pod. Indulge in relaxation and rejuvenation in our state-of-the-art natural spring water hot tubs. Immerse yourself in the warm, soothing waters while marveling at the surrounding nature. Whether you choose to unwind after a day of exploring or simply bask in the serenity of the countryside, our hot tubs provide the perfect sanctuary for your well-being. At Craig View, we believe in cherishing the simple pleasures in life. That's why we invite you to bring your own drink to the pods, allowing you to unwind and enjoy the tranquil ambiance in your own personal way. Sip on your favorite beverage as you take in the panoramic views or gather with loved ones for an evening of laughter and conversation under the starry sky.
